About Us

Bridging the gap between manufacturers and healthcare providers worldwide

Equalife Healthcare Corp, based in Orlando, Florida, is dedicated to connecting small and medium-sized manufacturers with distributors worldwide. With over 25 years of experience in international sales and 16 years specializing in medical devices, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner in the healthcare industry.

We offer a diverse portfolio of high-quality consumable products at competitive prices, ensuring healthcare providers have access to reliable, innovative solutions that improve patient outcomes.

Our strong, long-standing business relationships enable us to provide exceptional support and expertise, helping our partners navigate the complex healthcare marketplace with confidence.
